# Submission Metadata

- **Project Title**: PixTrap Reveals LLM Safety Calibration Gaps in Brazilian Pix Fraud
- **Author**: Patrick Passos
- **Affiliation**: Independent Researcher / GitHub: patrickpassosb
- **Regional Track**: Latin America
- **Sub-Track**: Technical safety / Locally-tailored AI safety evaluation
- **Summary**: PixTrap is a Brazilian Portuguese safety benchmark that evaluates whether large language models refuse Pix fraud and social-engineering prompts while still answering legitimate anti-fraud help requests. By pairing harmful instructions with benign near-neighbors, PixTrap measures both unsafe compliance and over-refusal. Evaluating five models across pt-BR and English reveals a modest 10-20% cross-language safety gap for 3 of 5 models and large differences in safe-redirect behavior.
- **Keywords**: AI Safety, Pix Fraud, Social Engineering, Brazilian Portuguese, Multilingual Evaluation, Over-Refusal, Calibration
