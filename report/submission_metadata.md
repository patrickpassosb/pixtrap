# Submission Metadata

- **Project Title**: PixTrap: Do Models Know When Pix Help Becomes Pix Fraud?
- **Author**: Patrick Passos
- **Affiliation**: Independent Researcher / GitHub: patrickpassos
- **Regional Track**: Latin America
- **Sub-Track**: Technical safety / Locally-tailored AI safety evaluation
- **Summary**: PixTrap is a Brazilian Portuguese safety benchmark that evaluates whether large language models refuse Pix fraud and social-engineering prompts while still answering legitimate anti-fraud help requests. By pairing harmful instructions with benign near-neighbors, PixTrap measures both unsafe compliance and over-refusal. Evaluating five models across pt-BR and English reveals significant gaps where English-centric safety testing fails to reflect local Brazilian vulnerabilities.
- **Keywords**: AI Safety, Pix Fraud, Social Engineering, Brazilian Portuguese, Multilingual Evaluation, Over-Refusal, Calibration
